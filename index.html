Privacy first
Your data stays yours
We built this for families. Here's exactly how your information is handled — no legal jargon.
🏦
No bank connections
RightOn Budget never connects to your bank. You enter your own numbers. No open banking credentials, no account read access — ever.
📧
No marketing emails
Your email is used only for account verification and login. We don't send newsletters, promotions, or share your information with third parties without your permission.
🚫
No ads, ever
Your financial data is never used for advertising or profiling. RightOn Budget is completely ad-free — your numbers are your business, not ours.
🔐
Private by design
Each household's data is completely isolated. Face ID on iOS means you're the only one who can open the app — even if someone else picks up your phone.
